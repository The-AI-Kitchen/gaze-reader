# GazeReader — Product vision

## FOR

Graduate students and researchers who read dense academic papers.

## WHO

Often work outside their core expertise and must **stop to look up references, methods, and background**—opening searches, other PDFs, and encyclopedic tabs—in a way that **fragments attention** and **slows progress on the paper they set out to read**.

## THE

GazeReader is a reading assistant web application.

## THAT

Keeps you **anchored in the article in front of you**: you get **grounded explanations and pointers** about the passage you’re on **without the usual round-trip** through browsers and side documents.

## UNLIKE

Copy-pasting into ChatGPT (or similar) **or** the default habit of **chasing citations and methods across the web** every time comprehension gaps appear.

## OUR PRODUCT

Uses your **gaze** (via webcam) to infer **which paragraph you’re focused on**, then answers with **that passage plus the full paper** as context—so help shows up **where your attention already is**.

## POWERED BY

Real-time gaze tracking + LLM comprehension grounded in specific passages.
