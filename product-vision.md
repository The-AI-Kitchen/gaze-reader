# GazeReader — Product vision

## FOR

Graduate students and researchers who read dense academic papers.

## WHO

Struggle with unfamiliar terminology and methods in papers outside their expertise.

## THE

GazeReader is a reading assistant web application.

## THAT

Lets you ask contextual questions about the passage you're reading without breaking flow.

## UNLIKE

ChatGPT or other AI tools where you copy-paste text and explain context.

## OUR PRODUCT

Tracks gaze via webcam, identifies your paragraph, answers with passage + paper context.

## POWERED BY

Real-time gaze tracking + LLM comprehension grounded in specific passages.
